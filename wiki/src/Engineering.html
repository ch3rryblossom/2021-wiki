Cyanobacteria is the green element, both figuratively and literally, of our project. Modelling its behaviour both in a pure culture and in the co culture matters a lot to the direction we put our attention to. We’ve used FBA and MoMA to predict the growth rates and sucrose production rates of cyanobacteria, both with and without salt stress. Dr.Costas Maranas was generous enough to provide the Genome scale metabolic model for Synechococcus elongatus UTEX 2973 from Mueller et al., 2017.


Modelling Cyanobacteria for optimum sucrose production


The first hurdle we encountered while modelling cyanobacteria was simulating salt stress. Literature says simulating something like salt stress alters a lot of reactions in the organism and not all of them are straight forward or even known, so we had to make a few assumptions to make the model work.
